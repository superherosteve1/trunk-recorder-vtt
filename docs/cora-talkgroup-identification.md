## Filing tips

- Prefer the city’s published CORA / open-records portal or email address for the Police Department or Technology / Radio Services custodian.
- Keep Denver and Aurora as **separate** requests.
- Start with the top 10–15 IDs; you can follow up with more later.
- Expect possible redaction or denial for “security” reasons; a Dist 7 / DIA routing question is still worth asking even if fleetmap rows are withheld.
- Activity counts above were sampled from this monitor’s history and will change; the IDs are what matter.

## Optional one-liner if they ask “why”

> These talkgroup IDs appear in publicly receivable trunking control-channel activity. I am requesting only their official labels/assignments for accurate public monitoring documentation, not encrypted content or cryptographic material.
